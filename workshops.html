    Workshop Topics

    First-Time Buyer Journey

    What You'll Learn:

    • Understanding mortgage types and terms
    • Deposit requirements and saving strategies
    • Government schemes (Help-to-Buy, Shared Ownership)
    • The buying process step-by-step
    • Hidden costs of homeownership
    • Mortgage protection essentials
    Ideal For: Anyone planning to purchase their first property within the next 2 years

    Pension Power

    What You'll Learn:

    • How workplace pensions work
    • Auto-enrolment explained
    • Finding lost pensions
    • Consolidation benefits and risks
    • Retirement income planning
    • State pension basics
    Ideal For: Anyone under 60 who wants to understand and optimize their pension

    Protection Planning

    What You'll Learn:

    • Types of insurance coverage
    • Life insurance vs critical illness
    • Income protection strategies
    • How much coverage you really need
    • Policy comparison tips
    • Common exclusions to watch for
    Ideal For: Families, homeowners, or primary breadwinners

    Investment Basics

    What You'll Learn:

    • Stocks, bonds, and funds explained
    • ISA advantages
    • Risk vs return understanding
    • Diversification strategies
    • Short vs long-term investing
    • Common investment mistakes
    Ideal For: Beginners with savings to invest or those wanting to understand investing

    Will Writing Essentials

    What You'll Learn:

    • Why everyone needs a will
    • What happens without a will
    • Inheritance tax basics
    • Guardian appointments
    • Executor responsibilities
    • Power of Attorney
