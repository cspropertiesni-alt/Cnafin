Insurance & Protection

Financial Protection When You Need It Most

You work all your life to build, grow and preserve your wealth, your future plans are based on your ability to earn an income. If that income stops through accident, ill health or death that future will look very different without a financial safety net to protect you and your family. The average claim length on an Income protection plan is 6 years. What position would you be in without an income for 6 years ?

Protection Options:

  • Life insurance
  • Critical illness cover
  • Income protection
  • Private medical insurance
  • Group death in service
  • Business protection
Important: The value of investments and any income from them can fall as well as rise and you may not get back the original amount invested

Peace of Mind: Ensure your mortgage payments, bills, and family's lifestyle are protected regardless of what life throws at you.
