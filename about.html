About Anderson Wealth Management

Our goal was, and always will be, to provide an independent financial planning and advice service to individuals and business owners across Northern Ireland. Advice that is built on long term planning and relationships.

Financial stress affects productivity, wellbeing, and quality of life. Our mission is to reduce that stress through education, personalised planning, and ongoing support. Whether you're buying your first home, planning for retirement, or protecting your family's future, we're here to guide you every step of the way.
